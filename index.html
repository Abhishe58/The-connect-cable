Welcome to Connect Cables

We have been in the wiring harness space for over three years.

We have been in the wiring harness space for over three years. We started with TML Indica & CV wiring harness as a job work with TATA YAZAKI and have been appreciated by them. The credentials can be seen below.

Currently we are assembling wiring harness for TATA YAZAKI NANO CAR as is clear from the photo gallery .

An ISO certified company ,we are a focused and progressive organization providing our customers with delight based on quality and timely delivery.

We have our exclusive premises in GIDC Gandhinagar, Capital of Gujarat with its excellent road and rail network, an excellent logistics support as well as adequate space for storage of items before and after assembly.

As a progressive company we target, in the future, wiring harnesses of entire cross section of vehicles both passenger cars as well as commercial vehicles .

Also wiring harnesses for white goods, medical diagnostics equipment, Electrical and electronics equipment.

The Evolution of Our Manufacturing Excellence

Jan 2010

Foundation Laid

Established our manufacturing unit in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, with a focus on quality wiring harness production.

Jan 2012

OEM Collaborations Begin

Started supplying to major OEMs, including Tata Motors and General Motors, following strict compliance standards.

Jan 2015

Infrastructure Expansion

Upgraded to include semi-automatic and automatic crimping, stripping, and cutting machines to scale production.

Jan 2018

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 certification, reinforcing our commitment to quality and process-driven operations.

Jan 2022

Export Operations Launched

Started delivering customized solutions for international markets across multiple sectors.

Jan 2024

Smart Manufacturing Initiative

Began implementation of process automation and data-driven efficiency tracking across all production lines.
